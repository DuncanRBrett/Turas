## Document Structure

Each brief follows a consistent, client-friendly format:

### Section 1: Executive Overview

-   **What This Module Does** - One-sentence summary
-   **What Problem Does It Solve?** - Business context
-   **How It Works** - High-level methodology (no jargon)

### Section 2: Deliverables & Technology

-   **What You Get** - Outputs and reports
-   **Technology Used** - R packages with plain-English explanations

### Section 3: Assessment

-   **Strengths** - What it does exceptionally well
-   **Limitations** - When NOT to use it
-   **Best Use Cases** - Ideal applications

### Section 4: Understanding the Analysis

-   **Statistical Concepts Explained** - Plain English definitions
-   **Example Outputs** - Real-world sample tables

-   **Quality & Reliability** - Production readiness
-   **What's Next** - Future enhancements roadmap
